About Us

The 2020-21 academic year marks the start of Oberoi International School’s 13th year of operation, and what a journey it has been to this point! The school opened its doors in August 2008 to an original, founding cohort of 38 students and by August 2020, the number of students enrolled at OIS had grown to over 2550 students from Nursery to Grade 12. From a first graduating class of just 15 students in 2011, 108 members of the Class of 2020 proudly received their diplomas in May. 13+ YEARS OVER 2550 STUDENTS ONE SCHOOL TWO CAMPUSES Another watershed development marked the opening of the 2017-18 academic year: Our second campus opened (to students in Nursery – Grade 4) on the Jogeshwari-Vikhroli Linking Road (or JVLR), just a couple of kilometres south of the original OGC campus. We have had to adjust our nomenclature to distinguish between our two campuses, so we now refer to the original campus in Goregaon East as our “Goregaon Campus/OGC Campus.”, reflecting its location within the Oberoi Garden City development and the new campus is now referred to as our “JVLR Campus,” reflecting its location on this main east-west artery.
